Planet Earth

  1. Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
  2. It is the only known planet to support life.
  3. Earth has a diameter of about 12,742 kilometers.
  4. It has one natural satellite, the Moon.
  5. Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen.
  • Earth is home to a diverse range of ecosystems, including forests, deserts, and oceans.
  • It has a rich variety of flora and fauna, with millions of species.
  • Earth's climate is influenced by its atmosphere and the Sun's energy.
  • Human activities have a significant impact on Earth's environment.
  • Efforts are being made globally to protect and preserve our planet for future generations.
Earth's Layers
Earth is composed of three main layers: the crust, mantle, and core.
Earth's Orbit
Earth orbits the Sun at an average distance of about 93 million miles (150 million kilometers).
Earth's Rotation
Earth rotates on its axis once every 24 hours, resulting in day and night.
Earth's Tilt
Earth is tilted on its axis by about 23.5 degrees, which causes the seasons.
